Academic Programs:

Undergraduate:

Lumos University offers a diverse range of undergraduate majors across various disciplines, including arts and humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, engineering, business, and more.

Graduate:

The graduate school at Lumos offers advanced degrees in fields such as medicine, law, business administration, engineering, arts, sciences, and humanities. These programs emphasize research.

Honors:

Exceptionally motivated students have the opportunity to participate in honors programs, allowing for more advanced coursework, personalized mentorship, and specialized research opportunities.

Dual-Degree:

Lumos collaborates with other leading universities to offer dual-degree programs that allow students to earn degrees from both institutions in less time than it would take to complete them separately.

Residential College

The residential college system fosters a close-knit community where students from different backgrounds and disciplines live together, fostering meaningful connections and lifelong friendships.

Clubs and Socities

With a wide array of student clubs and organizations, Lumos encourages students to pursue their passions beyond the classroom. Whether in the arts, sports, social justice, or academics, there's a group for everyone.

Fests

Lumos celebrates diversity through cultural events, workshops, and performances. These events provide opportunities for students to learn about and appreciate different cultures.

Outdoor Spaces

The campus boasts picturesque outdoor spaces, including gardens, walking paths, and recreational areas, providing students with places to relax, study, and connect with nature.
